# Track a person on X

Monitor any X (Twitter) account and get daily reports with translated summaries, top posts, and engagement analysis.

## Create your Mind

> Create a Mind that helps to track '[person or @handle]' Twitter posts.

**Name ideas:** ElonTracker, CompetitorWatchDog, ThoughtLeaderDigest

## Configure reporting

After your Mind is awakened, send these:

> Report once per day.

> Report your findings every day at 00:00 UTC.

> In the report, analyze the most viewed and most liked posts of the day.

> Summarize the tweets.

## What you get

- Translated updates (works across languages)
- Daily summary of all activity
- Top-performing post analysis (most viewed + most liked)
- Contextual analysis of why posts performed well

## Skill required

None — built-in capability.
