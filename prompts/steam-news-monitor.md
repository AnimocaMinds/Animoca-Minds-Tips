# Monitor Steam news

Set up an AI agent to monitor Steam's RSS feed and deliver daily news summaries to Discord or email.

## Create your Mind

> Create the Mind now.

Set its name (e.g. "Steam Scout"), persona ("gaming news expert"), and specialty ("RSS monitoring and Discord posting").

## Equip the Steam skill

> Load the artifact Steam_Web_API_v1

If the artifact ID has changed:

> List available Steam artifacts.

## Example instructions

> Give me daily news from Steam covering Action, Action RPG, and RPG games.

> Let me know the Steam service status around the Hong Kong region.

> Post a daily Steam news summary to my Discord channel every morning at 9am.

> Only include news about games tagged 'Souls-like' or 'Open World'.

## Test it

> Generate a sample Steam news summary and post it to my Discord channel.

> Generate a sample Steam news summary and email it to me.

## What you need

- An Animoca Minds account
- A Discord server with bot permissions (for Discord delivery)
