# Trade NFTs on OpenSea

Enable your Mind to analyze NFT markets and execute trades autonomously through OpenSea APIs.

## Create your Mind

Set your Mind's specialty to **Investing**.

> New users: go to animocaminds.ai, enter your email, reply to the Concierge AI, name your Mind, and set specialty to "Investing".

## Equip the OpenSea skill

> Equip yourself with the skill 'OpenSea_Trade_Analysis' (ID: 686F1D76-BA1A-F111-AD1D-0EA9A5017E89)

## What you need

- Blockchain wallets on Ethereum or Base with funds for gas fees
- An OpenSea API key (stored encrypted in your private vault)
- Cognition credits allocated to your Mind

## How it works

Your Mind follows a four-step execution loop for every trade:

1. **Think** — analyzes collections, listings, and pricing signals
2. **Build** — constructs a Seaport 1.6 transaction payload
3. **Verify** — audits price validity, expiration, and gas thresholds
4. **Ship** — signs via EIP-712 and broadcasts to the blockchain
